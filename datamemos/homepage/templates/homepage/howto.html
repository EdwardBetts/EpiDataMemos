How it Works

Each "chart" is a table of data. The controls at the top of the page illustrate how that table is being display in the bar chart.

You can navigate a chart by using the controls at the top of the page.

You can use your mouse to interact directly with the chart, and bring up details about any point in the table.

As you navigate the chart, notes will organize themselves to show you the most relevant information.

To see what a note references, hover or click on the note's bookmark.

To add a comment to the chart, click 'Add Note' in the top left corner of the screen.

When you add a comment, you will be sent an email with a permanent link to view the note, and a permanent link to edit the note.

Optionally, you can post a private note - in case you only want people with the link to see your comment.

You can share any note by clicking the "Share" link at the bottom the note.

You can share any screen by clicking the "Share" link in the top left corner of the screen.
